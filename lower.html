Lower Body

Separate lower-body page for legs, glutes, power and athletic base.

Lower Body Plan

Quad Focus

  • Squat - 4x5-8
  • Leg Press - 4x10-12
  • Walking Lunge - 3x10 per leg
  • Leg Extension - 3x12-15
  • Calf Raise - 4x12-20

Posterior Focus

  • Romanian Deadlift - 4x6-10
  • Hip Thrust - 4x8-12
  • Leg Curl - 4x10-15
  • Step-Up - 3x10 per leg
  • Seated Calf Raise - 4x12-20

Technique

  • Knees track with toes.
  • Use full range that you can control.
  • Brace before squats and hinges.
  • Drive through the full foot, not only toes.
