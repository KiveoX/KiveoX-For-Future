Back Training

Separate back page for width, thickness, posture and lower-back stability.

Back Goals

Lat WidthUpper Back ThicknessPostureLower Back Stability

Train vertical pulls for width, rows for thickness, rear delts for balance and hip-hinge work for posterior-chain strength.

Back Plan 2x Weekly

Day A - Width

  • Pull-Ups or Lat Pulldown - 4x6-10
  • Single-Arm Cable Row - 4x8-12
  • Straight-Arm Pulldown - 3x12-15
  • Face Pull - 3x15-20

Day B - Thickness

  • Barbell Row - 4x6-8
  • Chest-Supported Row - 4x8-12
  • Romanian Deadlift - 3x6-10
  • Rear Delt Fly - 3x15-20

Technique

  • Pull elbows down for lats, not just hands.
  • Keep ribs controlled and avoid swinging.
  • Use full stretch and controlled squeeze.
  • For rows, keep chest tall and shoulder blades controlled.
