Core / V-Line Plan

Separate page for lower abs, obliques, deep core control and visible V-Line foundation.

Most Important Point

The V-Line is not built by ab exercises alone. It becomes visible through lower body fat, strong lower abs, trained obliques, posture and consistency.

Rule: Train the foundation now, but measure waist trend and nutrition too.

Level System

Beginner

  • Dead Bug - 3x10 per side
  • Reverse Crunch - 3x8-12
  • Plank - 3x20-30 sec
  • Side Plank - 2x15-25 sec

Intermediate

  • Kneeling Ab Rollout - 3x6-10
  • Hanging Knee Raise - 3x8-12
  • Russian Twist - 3x10 per side
  • Pallof Press - 3x10-12 per side

Advanced

  • Ab Rollout - 4x10
  • Hanging Leg Raise - 4x8-12
  • Cable Crunch - 4x10-15
  • Side Plank Hip Dip - 3x10-15 per side

2-3x Weekly Plan

Day A

  • Ab Rollouts - 4x10
  • Hanging Knee Raises - 4x8-12
  • Russian Twists - 4x10 per side
  • Side Plank - 3x30-45 sec

Day B

  • Cable Crunches - 4x10-15
  • Reverse Crunches - 4x10-15
  • Ab Rollouts - 3x8-10
  • Pallof Press - 3x12 per side
Leave at least one rest day between hard core sessions.

Technique Rules

  • No momentum.
  • Brace before every rep.
  • Do not let the lower back collapse during rollouts.
  • Stop or regress if lower back pain appears.
  • Control first, reps second, load third.
