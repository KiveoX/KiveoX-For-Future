Booty Workout

Separate glute page for gym growth, home activation, pump and technique control.

Booty Growth Rules

Train 3-4x weeklyProtein highHydrationSleepProgressive overload

Glutes grow through training stimulus, food, recovery and patience. Use both heavy gym movements and controlled home activation.

Glute Growth Slim Thigh Focus

1.Hip Thrusts
4 sets8-12 reps

Drive through the heels, tuck the pelvis slightly and squeeze the glutes at the top without overextending the lower back.

Glute Bridges
4 sets12-15 reps

Keep the movement controlled, push through the heels and pause at the top to maximize glute tension.

Single-Leg Hip Thrusts
3 sets10-12 per leg

Keep the hips level and focus on squeezing the working glute instead of pushing through the thigh.

Cable Kickbacks
3 sets12-15 per leg

Keep hips stable, avoid swinging and squeeze the glute at the end of each rep.

5.Abductor Machine
4 sets15-20 reps

Lean slightly forward, move slowly and focus on the side glutes instead of using momentum.

Seated Banded Abductions
3 sets20-25 reps

Keep constant tension on the band and open the knees slowly while keeping the torso stable.

Frog Pumps
3 sets20-30 reps

Place the soles of your feet together, knees out, and lift the hips while squeezing the glutes hard at the top.

Cable Pull-Throughs
3 sets12-15 reps

Hinge from the hips, keep the back flat and drive the hips forward using the glutes.

Romanian Deadlifts
3 sets8-10 reps

Push hips back, keep knees only slightly bent and feel the stretch in the glutes and hamstrings.

10.Glute-Focused Back Extensions
3 sets12-15 reps

Round the upper back slightly, keep the chin tucked and squeeze the glutes to lift the body.

Reverse Hyperextensions
3 sets12-15 reps

Lift the legs by squeezing the glutes, not by arching the lower back.

Standing Cable Abductions
3 sets15-20 per leg

Move the leg out to the side slowly and keep the hips square to target the side glutes.

Side-Lying Leg Raises
3 sets15-20 per side

Lift the top leg with control, keep toes slightly turned down and focus on the glute medius.

Donkey Kicks
3 sets15-20 per leg

Keep the core tight and press the heel upward while squeezing the glute at the top.

15.Quadruped Hip Circles
3 sets12-15 per direction

Move slowly through the hip, keep the torso stable and focus on controlled glute activation.

Smith Machine Hip Thrusts
4 sets8-12 reps

Keep your chin slightly tucked, drive through the heels and squeeze the glutes hard at the top without arching your lower back.

Dumbbell Glute Bridges
4 sets12-15 reps

Place the dumbbell over your hips, push through the heels and pause at the top for strong glute tension.

Banded Glute Bridges
3 sets15-20 reps

Keep the band tight by pressing the knees slightly outward while lifting the hips and squeezing the glutes.

Kas Glute Bridges
4 sets10-12 reps

Use a shorter range of motion than a hip thrust, stay controlled and keep constant tension in the glutes.

20.B-Stance Hip Thrusts
3 sets10-12 per side

Place one foot slightly forward as support and let the back leg do most of the work for better glute focus.

B-Stance Romanian Deadlifts
3 sets10-12 per side

Keep most weight on the front leg, push the hips back and feel the stretch mainly in the glute and hamstring.

Single-Leg Romanian Deadlifts
3 sets10-12 per leg

Move slowly, keep the hips square and reach the back leg behind you while loading the working glute.

45-Degree Glute Back Extensions
4 sets12-15 reps

Turn the toes slightly outward, round the upper back a little and lift by squeezing the glutes instead of the lower back.

Banded Kickbacks
3 sets15-20 per leg

Keep the core tight, avoid swinging and press the heel back while squeezing the working glute.

25.Machine Glute Kickbacks
3 sets12-15 per leg

Keep the movement slow and controlled, pause at the top and do not let the lower back take over.

Standing Banded Abductions
3 sets20 reps per side

Stand tall, move the leg out to the side and keep the hips stable to target the side glutes.

Leaning Cable Abductions
3 sets15-20 per side

Lean slightly away from the cable machine and lift the leg out with control to hit the glute medius.

Hip Thrust Abduction Pulses
3 sets20-25 reps

Hold the hips high in a bridge position and pulse the knees outward while keeping glute tension.

Fire Hydrants
3 sets15-20 per leg

Keep the torso still and lift the knee out to the side without rotating the hips too much.

30.Weighted Frog Pumps
3 sets20-30 reps

Place weight over the hips, keep the soles together and squeeze the glutes with every short rep.

Prone Glute Squeezes
3 sets20-30 reps

Lie face down, bend the knees slightly and squeeze the glutes to lift the thighs gently off the floor.

Stability Ball Hamstring Curls
3 sets12-15 reps

Keep the hips lifted, curl the ball toward you and maintain glute tension throughout the movement.

Hamstring Curl Machine
3 sets10-15 reps

Use controlled reps to train the hamstrings without making the quads the main focus.

Low Cable Glute Raises
3 sets12-15 per leg

Attach the cable low, raise the leg slightly back and up, and keep the movement small and glute-focused.

35.Wall Glute Bridge Holds
3 sets30-45 sec hold

Place your feet on a wall, lift the hips and hold while squeezing the glutes without pushing through the quads.
