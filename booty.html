Booty Workout

Separate glute page for gym growth, home activation, pump and technique control.

Booty Growth Rules

Train 3-4x weeklyProtein highHydrationSleepProgressive overload

Glutes grow through training stimulus, food, recovery and patience. Use both heavy gym movements and controlled home activation.

Gym Glute Growth

Hip Thrusts
4 sets8-12 reps

Drive through heels, pause at the top and squeeze glutes without overextending the lower back.

Bulgarian Split Squats
3 sets10-12 per leg

Keep the front foot stable and move slowly. Strong for glutes and quads.

Romanian Deadlifts
4 sets8-10 reps

Push hips back, keep back flat and feel the stretch in hamstrings and glutes.

Cable Kickbacks
3 sets12-15 per leg

No swinging. Keep hips stable and squeeze the glute at the end.
